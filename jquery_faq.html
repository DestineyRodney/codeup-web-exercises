Lemur FAQs:

Ring-tailed Lemur
The ring tailed lemur's tail is longer than its body!
Red Ruffed Lemur
Red ruffed lemurs have six bottom teeth known as a “tooth comb”. They use it to groom, or comb, their fur and to help pull the skin off fruit..
Mongoose Lemur
This lemur weighs only about three to four pounds.
Red-bellied Lemur
Although they are named red-bellied, it is only the males who have this feature, the females have a white underbelly.
Silky Sifaka Lemur
This lemur has a creamy white coat, due to which these animals are otherwise called 'angels of the forest'.
White-fronted Brown Lemur
They are cathemeral, which means that these animals are active at varying times throughout the day and night.
Northern Bamboo Lemur
heir noses and faces are smaller than many other lemurs.
Mittermeier’s Mouse Lemur
Mittermeier's mouse lemur is the smallest of the east coast mouse lemurs
Black Lemur
The black lemur lives in groups of 2 to 15 members, including approximately equal numbers of males and females.
Indri
The indri is a vertical clinger and leaper and thus holds its body upright when traveling through trees or resting in branches.

    Ring-Tailed Lemur Additional Facts

  • Male ring tailed lemurs put smells, from glands in their bottoms, on their tail and wave it at rivals. It’s known as ‘stink fighting’!
  • The ring tailed lemur is used as a symbol for Madagascar and for endangered animals on the island, because it’s so well-known.
  • King Julien, the most famous ring tailed lemur, likes to move it, move it!
  • Endangered

    Red Ruffed Lemur Additional Facts

  • Red ruffed lemurs love fruit. But they love figs the best. They will even fight over them!
  • Red ruffed lemurs have a long claw on the second toe of their back leg which they use for brushing their fluffy coats.
  • Red ruffed lemurs are one of the biggest primates living on Madagascar.
  • Endangered

    Mongoose Lemur Additional Facts

  • Live in pairs with offspring
  • Reach sexual maturity in 2 years
  • At sexual maturity (2.5-3.5 years old), offspring are encouraged to leave the family group by the parents.
  • Gestation: 126 days
